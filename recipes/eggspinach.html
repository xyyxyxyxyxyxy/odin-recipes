Eggs With Spinach

Description

This very simple recipe will result in a quick, healthy, and warm dish.

No less than 10 minutes required for a cheap and filling meal.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Spinach
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs, add salt and milk before scrambling
  2. Melt butter in a pan, Med. heat
  3. Place spinach in pan, still Med. heat
  4. Once spinach is cooked down and a darker green color, pour scrambled eggs into pan
  5. Keep stirring mix until eggs cooked as desired
