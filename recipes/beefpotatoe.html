Beef and Potatoes

Description

Simple and easy dinner, cheap nutritous and filling

Total cook time for 4 days worth is >1hr.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 4 Sweet Potatoes
  • 2 lb Organic Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 430, wash potatoes while heating.
  2. Put washed potatoes on pan and into over, set timer for 35 minutes.
  3. Melt butter in pan for ground beef
  4. Place ground beef in pan on medium heat, add salt and soy sauce to taste.
  5. Slow cook ground beef until no longer pink, should be ready at the same time as potatoes
